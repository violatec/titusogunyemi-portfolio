Our Story

A first generation engineering student, a father and husband turned love for plant, beauty, and colors into a family-owned landscaping business in Houston,

Texas. Titus is known to be an advocate of continuous learning, an attribute that have made him turned many passions into income generating skills.

Additionally, Titus is a dual major, Senior in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Texas Southern University, the institution where he first conceived

the idea of investing into landscape designing business. Titus is always fascinated by natural form and beauty of nature. His love for God’s creativity as found

in nature led him into having a strong interest in plants. From 2015 to till date, Titus have been actively involved in inquiry-based garden-landscape design

through community service and public education. Titus’ works have contributed enormously to the beautification of his local community through partnership

with the leadership of city council.
